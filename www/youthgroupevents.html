Events & Ministries for the Youth Group

Youth Group Education

Middle School and High School members meet for Christian formation classes every Sunday at 10:00 a.m. from September to June.

Youth Group

The Youth Group meets for activities, events, special trips and community projects throughout the year. Separate activities are planned for each age group.

Youth Group Steering Committee

Youth Group parents meets twice a year to plan youth activities and events.

Antique Show Dealer Move In and Move Out

Every year members of the Youth Group assist dealers by helping them to move in and move out on the day before the show opens and after the show closes.

Antiques Show Luncheon Servers

Members of the Youth Group serve lunch to patrons of the show on Friday, Saturday and Sunday on the patio.

Halloween Party

Usually held the Sunday before Halloween and hosted by the Youth Group following the 10 o’clock service. The younger children of the parish come in costume and are treated to a variety of activities and surprises.

Scholarship Fund

The Scholarship Fund is available to all graduating high school seniors involved in the St. Mary’s Youth Program. Applications are required. Contributions to this fund are gratefully received.

Spaghetti Dinner Scholarship Fundraiser

This special all parish fund raiser is held in May to help raise money for the Youth Group Scholarship Fund. Scholarship checks are awarded to selected graduating seniors at this event.
